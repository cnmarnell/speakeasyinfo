Feedback is the bottleneck.

A professor with 120 students can't give meaningful, detailed feedback on every speech. So students present once or twice, get a grade, and move on — never learning why.

Meanwhile, the tools that do exist are built for enterprise sales teams or individual consumers. None are designed for the way education actually works — with classes, assignments, rubrics, and progress over a semester.

Until now.

How It Works

Three Steps to Better Speakers

Speakeasy fits seamlessly into existing classroom workflows. No training required.

1

Record

Students record their speech directly in the app — from a dorm room, library, or classroom. Just hit record and speak.

2

AI Analyzes

Our AI transcribes the speech and evaluates it against your custom rubric criteria with detailed, actionable feedback.

3

Improve

Students see exactly where to improve, track progress across assignments, and build confidence with every iteration. Professors see it all.

Practice Without the Pressure
